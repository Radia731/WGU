City of Angeles

Los Angeles, often called LA, is the largest city in California and the second most populous city in the U.S. Known for its sunny weather, sprawling urban landscape, and deep ties to film and entertainment, LA is a global cultural and creative hub.

More About Los Angeles

  • Nickname: City of Angels
  • Founded: September 4, 1781
  • Population: ~3.8 million
  • Mayor: Karen Bass (as of 2025)
