Welcome to Los Angeles

City of Angeles

Los Angeles, often called LA, is the largest city in California and the second most populous city in the U.S. Known for its sunny weather, sprawling urban landscape, and deep ties to film and entertainment, LA is a global cultural and creative hub.

More About Los Angeles

  • Nickname: City of Angels
  • Founded: September 4, 1781
  • Population: ~3.8 million
  • Mayor: Karen Bass (as of 2025)

Fun Fact

  • 🎬 Los Angeles is the entertainment capital of the world—home to Hollywood, the Oscars, and countless movie studios.
  • 🌆 LA has over 100 different neighborhoods, each with its own unique vibe—from Venice Beach to Koreatown.
  • 🌴 It’s one of the only cities where you can ski and surf in the same day (with a little planning, of course).
  • 🌎 More than 200 languages are spoken in LA—it’s one of the most linguistically diverse cities on Earth.
  • 🎨 LA has the most museums per capita in the U.S.—over 100, including The Getty and LACMA.
  • 🚗 LA's car culture is iconic—it's home to the first freeway in the U.S. and *legendary* traffic.
  • 🏀 Staples Center (now Crypto.com Arena) is home to two NBA teams—the Lakers and the Clippers.
  • 🍔 The world’s first cheeseburger was reportedly served in LA in the 1920s.
